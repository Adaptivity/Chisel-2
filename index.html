What is Chisel?
Chisel adds a huge variety of static blocks into the game. This mod is useful for people who enjoy the construction aspect of Minecraft. In adition to blocks Chisel also adds a few useful items, The Ball O' Moss and the Cloud in a bottle.
Downloads

Items

Chisel adds a few items to help you decorate your world and add a little more to those builds.
The Chisel

The Chisel does everything in this mod, You right click to open a gui where you can transform your blocks into different looks and styles. You can also left click it on a block in the world to change the style of it without having to mine and replace it.

Ball O' Moss

The Ball O' Moss is a thrown item that can be used on anything that can grow mossy, it will scatter some moss around in the area of where it lands, and allows you to get some randomization in your mossy builds.

Cloud in a Bottle

The Cloud in a Bottle is a thrown item that can be used to create a cloud in the area where it lands. The cloud blocks can be chiseled into bricks and other designs to add a cool effect to your builds, or just leave it as a cloud.

Smashing Rock

Smashing Rock is a thrown item that can be used to decay any blocks it hits in an are affect. Cobblestone decays to gravel, and gravel to sand. This can be used to make neat pathways throughout your build.
